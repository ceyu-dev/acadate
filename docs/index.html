About Acadate

Acadate is a React Native app that keeps track of your academic calendar so you can plan your trips, dates, and other extracurricular activities ahead of time without needing to open that god-forsaken university portal. Acadate lets you keep track of multiple university calendars simultaneously. Want to hang out with friends from other universities?

Just check for gaps.
